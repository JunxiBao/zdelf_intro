About the App

Health Record Assistant is a personal health data management application designed to help users track and record their health metrics, symptoms, diet, and other wellness indicators.

Key Features

  • 📊 Health metrics recording (temperature, blood pressure, etc.)
  • 🍎 Diet and nutrition tracking
  • 🤒 Symptom logging and monitoring
  • 📈 Data visualization and trends
  • 📤 Data export and backup
  • 🤖 AI-powered health insights

Important Notice

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer: This app is for personal health record-keeping only and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or advice. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical concerns.
