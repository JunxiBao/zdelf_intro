Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

Our app (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects limited personal information to enhance the quality of AI-based health analysis.

1. What We Collect

We collect your age, email address or phone number, and health data (such as temperature, blood pressure, symptoms, diet records, and other health metrics) with your explicit consent.

2. Why We Collect It

Your age and health data are used to provide personalized health insights via our trusted AI system (similar to ChatGPT) for personal record-keeping and analysis. Your email or phone number is used for login and account-related communication.

3. Data Use and Sharing

We do not sell or share your data for advertising or any commercial purposes. Health data is used strictly for personal record-keeping and AI analysis. Data is stored securely and can be deleted upon request.

4. Security

We use encrypted connections and follow best practices to protect your data.

5. Your Rights

  • View or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Medical Disclaimer

Important: This app is for personal health record-keeping only and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or advice. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

7. Children

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained until you delete your account or request data deletion. You can export your data at any time through the app's export feature.

9. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at junxibao@junxibao.com
